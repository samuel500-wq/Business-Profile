🌍 About Us
Velon Technologies Rwanda is a technology company dedicated to developing innovative digital solutions that address real-world challenges across Africa and beyond.
Our mission is to leverage emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Web Development, and Data Analytics to create impactful products that improve lives, empower businesses, and support sustainable development.

🚀 Featured Projects
🌱 AI Greenshield
AI-powered crop disease detection platform designed to assist farmers by identifying plant diseases through image recognition.
🌾 Rumbuka AI
An intelligent agricultural platform that combines AI-driven insights, crop monitoring, and disease detection technologies.
🤖 Smart Omni Assistant (SOA-1)
A next-generation intelligent assistant capable of providing information, automation, learning support, and smart recommendations.
♻️ EcoForge
A sustainability-focused solution that converts plastic waste into valuable construction materials such as tiles and bricks.
📰 Zandani
A modern digital journalism and media platform aimed at delivering reliable news and empowering content creators.
🏨 Tourism & Hospitality Platform
A comprehensive booking and marketing ecosystem connecting travelers with tourism and hospitality service providers.
🚔 Rwanda Crime Prevention and Intelligence System (RCPIS)
An AI-powered situational awareness and crime prevention platform utilizing machine learning and computer vision technologies.
